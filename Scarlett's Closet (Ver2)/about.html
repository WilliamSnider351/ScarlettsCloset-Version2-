Scarlett’s Closet

We use a model focused approach in our sales. We only sell products worn by models, such as Scarlett Johansson and Kristen Stewart. Founded in 2025, we are now a fast-growing online fashion boutique.

Scarlett’s Closet focuses on classic styling and high fashion. We offer women’s clothing items that range from casual streetwear to dresses for the most formal of events.

Scarlett’s Closet offers women’s ready-to-wear, dresses, skirts, boots, shoes, and bags. These products are offered on our e-commerce sites and at boutiques in some of the world’s biggest cities.

Scarlett’s Closet is led by William Snider as CEO. Our design team is guided by Michael Vitale. Michael has been with us as Chief Creative Officer since May 5, 2025.
